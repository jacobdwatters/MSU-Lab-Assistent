Go over materials checklist. Ensure that you have all required materials.
Weigh out 3.5 grams of fine copper powder. Make sure to strip the scale before weighing.
Put the copper powder into a 250ml beaker.
Pour 100ml of glacial acetic acid into the beaker.
Using a pipette, mesure 10ml of 10% concentration hydrogen poroxide as a catalyst.
Slowly add the hydrogen poroxide to the beaker. The reaction is exothermic and can be vigourus so be carefull.
Note the solution should change colors. What compound has just been formed?
